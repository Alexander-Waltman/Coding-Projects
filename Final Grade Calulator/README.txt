This script calculates what your final grade would be if you completed no other assignments and also what you need on the final exam to get your desired grade.
It reads this data from a .txt file where you input all the categories that impact your final grade and they're weights and the grades on them.

FILE FORMAT:
Name, Weight (decimal), Grade (decimal)

EXAMPLE:
Presentation, .1, .95
Midterm, .2, .85
s
This means the a presentation is worth 10% of your grade and you got a 95%, also the midterm is worth 20% of the grade and you got an 85% on it
